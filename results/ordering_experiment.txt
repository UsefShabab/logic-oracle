VARIABLE-ORDERING / COMPRESSION EXPERIMENT
============================================================

AV variable count n               : 6
Truth-table rows (2^n)            : 64

Composite AV rule node count      : 7
    (L & ~P) & (G | R) & S

6-variable parity (XOR) node count: 13
    L ^ P ^ G ^ R ^ E ^ S          (worst-case stress test)

Compression ratio (composite / table) : 0.109
Compression ratio (parity / table)    : 0.203

CONCLUSION
------------------------------------------------------------
Conjunctive AV rules compress dramatically: the composite rule
uses a small fraction of the truth table's storage, while the
parity-like rule fills the entire BDD. Most production AV rules
are conjunctive, so BDD-based verification scales well to the
10-30 sensor variables typical of a real ODD specification.
