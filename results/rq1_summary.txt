INTERPRETATION
--------------------------------------------------
The LLM parser handles all 14 sentences correctly because
each one falls within standard AV-spec language. The regex
baseline drops to 67%% at depth 3 (it routes 'neither/nor'
through its OR branch by mistake) and 75%% at depth 4. The
gap between the two lines is the practical value of the LLM,
but the BDD layer remains the only formal correctness
guarantee: even an LLM that reaches 100%% on this benchmark
can fail on adversarial inputs not in the training distribution.
